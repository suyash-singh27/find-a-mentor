Mentorship for Engineers!

I've had engineering students reach out to me for mentorship / project-recommendations / problem-solving in general. While I try my best to help them, I frequently find myself either incapable or too busy to help them - and often end up referring them to a more suitable mentor. 

I have compiled a list of good engineers (mostly with CS background) who are more than happy to help their juniors! Most of these are either pursuing higher education, or are corporate-slaves. They are willing to put in some hours to help you grow :)

If you believe you can provide mentorship to engineering students, and are available for a few hours every month - please fill this google form. I'd be more than happy to add you as a mentor. Thanks!
